About

The person behind the pixels

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 6+ years of experience leading design for mobile and web products across maritime, healthcare, education, sustainability, and finance.

I believe great design isn't about beautiful screens — it's about reducing friction for the people who actually use the product. Whether I'm running a design sprint with a 22-person team or working one-on-one with a founder, I bring the same discipline: understand the problem deeply before jumping to solutions.

When I'm not designing, I'm trekking trails or playing guitar — both hobbies that teach you that a good process beats rushing to the destination every time.
